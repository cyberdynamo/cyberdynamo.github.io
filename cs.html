Computer Science and Engineering

To create the most conducive environment for quality academic and research oriented undergraduate and postgraduate education in computer science and engineering and prepare the students for a globalised technological society and orient them towards serving the society.


  1. To impart high quality professional training at the postgraduate and undergraduate level with an emphasis on basic principles of computer science and engineering.
  2. To establish nationally and internationally recognized research centres and expose the students to broad research experience.
  3. To impart moral and ethical values, and interpersonal skills to the students.
  4. To empower the students with the required skills to solve the complex technological problems of modern society and also provide them with a framework for promoting collaborative and multidisciplinary activities.
